OOoXAdESSigIT

An OpenOffice.org extension to digitally sign OpenOffice.org native document according to Italian standard procedure. It's Free, Libre, Open Source Sofware.

.

OOoXAdESSignIT (friendly name: OXSIT) is an OpenOffice extension, that is, a pluggable sw module for the popular office automation suite. The goal is to manage electronic signatures (signing, verifying, timestamping) according to the XAdES specification, making the user able to produce legally valid documents according to the italian law.

.

OpenOffice already provides a digital signature functionality, but following the more general XMLSig specification; XAdES is a particular specialization of XMLSig for human-readable digital document signature, complying with European Union rules.

.

CNIPA is the source of technical regulations for the Information Technology in Italy (for the Public Administration primarily, but often also for all the other sectors). In 2006 CNIPA admitted XAdES between the legally valid digital signature formats, along with PKCS7 and PDF.

.

Digital Signature appears also in the ODF 1.2 drafts, which started to be implemented in OpenOfficefrom version 3.0, and this project will also maintain a close alignement in this regard.

For the sake of producing high grade electronic signatures, utilization of cryptographic tokens and qualified certificates is required. So a special attention is devoted to support the so-named SSCD (Secure Signature Creation Devices), like SmartCards, who are currently spreading around in Europe.
At start PKCS11 support will be the main goal, but preserving the generality of approach needed for easy integration of other specifications, like PKCS15.
The project will leverage on the experiences emerged from other Open Source projects like j4sign, OpenSignPdf and OpenSC.

A great effort will be also put in providing a valid user interface, simple to use but also granting to the user a strict control on the many delicate aspects of digital signature and verification.
